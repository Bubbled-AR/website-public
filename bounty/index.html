Bubbled Bounty Campaign

We are operating our bounty campaign, so participants from the community can help ensure the Bubbled platform achieves true decentralisation and in exchange, receive BBL as a bounty reward.

We have 400m BBL tokens minted in total. As a bounty reward we will allocate 20m BBL from all coins minted, to be earned via community-led projects.

We ask that after signing up, you help inform others about the Bubbled TGE in the crypto-community as widely as possible by following these simple instructions.

By completing bounty tasks you will receive the following bounty-stakes:

Terms & Conditions

In order to participate in the Bubbled bounties program:

  • Join the bounty program by completing the following form.
  • You must be a verified Telegram User.
  • Violation of any of the terms and conditions that are set out in this document will result in termination from the bounties program, and as a result you won’t receive any tokens.

The bounty will last the period from the pre-registration, pre-sale and TGE, from now until 31 May 2018.

Your account must be legitimate, it cannot be a bot, software, or otherwise. (Registered via Bounty Registration form on website).

Facebook

Only one user per account. We have articles that you may share. We also invite you to share your own original content. All posts should include the #BuildonBubbled hashtag After you make your post, you must keep it up for the duration of the TGE.

Twitter

Your profile must have at least 250 followers. Only one user per twitter account. You can either retweet Bubbled’s tweets about the TGE, or you can tweet your own original tweets. You must include the #BuildonBubbled hashtag. We’d expect you to post at least 5 times a week. After you make your post, you must keep it up for the duration of the TGE.

Instagram

Your profile must have at least 500 followers and be public. You can either share pre-made posts from our bank of content, or post original pictures or videos using the hashtag (#BuildOnBubbled or #Bubbleverse), and tag our TGE’s Instagram channel @seebubbled in the post.

After you make your post, you must keep it up for the duration of the TGE.

Original Blog Posts

Articles must exceed 500 words and must be well-researched and thorough. Once created, blog posts must be distributed via social channels.

Upon completion all reposts made by all participants will be calculated and divided to bounty participants accordingly.

When can you expect to receive tokens?

You will receive your tokens in late March/ early April 2018 to your registered Bubbled account, after the conclusion of the TGE.
