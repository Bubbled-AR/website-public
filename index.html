Welcome to the #Bubbleverse

A registrar platform to purchase and own real-world locations on the blockchain.

Or just rent them. Place any content you like: 3D graphics, video, images, rich-text, or social media posts, all viewable in real-time on Bubbled's augmented reality landscape.

A platform for Augmented Reality dApps, powered by the Ethereum blockchain.

Use Bubbled's technology in your own projects to create an augmented reality where users can create, experience, and monetize content and applications in real-world geographical locations.

Fully customizable and decentralized. Campaign to each specific user base however you want.

The #Bubbleverse is permanently owned by you, the Bubbled community, so you have full control over your own creations and purchases.

Why Bubbled?

The emerging AR ecosystem lacks governance and structure.

As an emergent technology, it needs clear definitions of what sharing branded content spaces are, who owns them, and an economy to make it work.

Avoiding repeat mistakes suffered by traditional digital advertisement systems.

Preventing fraudulent behavior such as adbots and ensuring your content cannot be tampered with are part of Bubbled's core priorities.

Current solutions are broken or simply non-existent.

Augmented reality and blockchain are disruptive technologies and people need a method to make sure their content reaches the right audiences. Bubbled's structure solves that.

Roadmap

  • Bubbled Founded

    Q3 June 2017

    Bubbled is conceptualized as a result from the widespread debate: ‘How can we build and implement a system of governance for AR content?’

  • Token Generation Event, Bubbled Alpha and Branded Beta Released

    Q2 2018

    Launch Bubbled’s land registration tool with beta product, Branded to serve AR content to real-world locations selected by registrar tool.

  • BBL Token Pre-Sale

    2nd April 2018

    Presale start date to raise 2,732 ETH ($1.337m approx.) to reach Soft Cap.

  • Main BBL Token Sale

    May 2018

    Token Generation Event to sell remaining tokens worth 28,916 ETH (approx. $12m) to reach Hard Cap.

  • Distribution of BBL token to TGE participants

    June - July 2018

    Distribution of BBL token to TGE participants and Bounty Campaign counts and distribution Q2.

  • Launch of beta desktop demand side portal and dApp’s Branded and cLand

    Q3 September 2018

    As development continues strong into next year, we will roll out a desktop version of the Bubbled portal, alongside the well-anticipated cLand beta and Branded full release.

  • Launch first music concert in AR (TBC)

    Q3 September 2018

    The first of many public events to be experienced within the Bubbleverse.

  • Launch of third dApp, 'Workable'

    Q1 February 2019

    Workable uses AR to easily identify new employee candidates and also promotes social economy methods such as the formation of ‘WorkSquares’ a virtual meetup hub of likeminded individuals and skill-sets bought together by Bubbled’s Heat Maps.
